Trend to Watch

The U.S. strategy of controlling AI development through export restrictions is being stress-tested in real time. China's GLM-5.2 matching American models on cybersecurity — while being freely available to anyone — suggests that open-source releases from well-resourced labs can route around geopolitical controls. Meanwhile, the drumbeat of new model releases (GPT-5.6, Grok 4.5) shows U.S. labs are accelerating too. The competitive dynamic is speeding up on both sides. This week, watch for how the U.S. government responds to the GLM-5.2 claims — any policy reaction or export control tightening would be a signal that officials see this as a genuine inflection point, not just a PR move from a Chinese lab.
