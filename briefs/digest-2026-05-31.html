Trend to Watch

The gap between AI capability demos and agentic task performance is becoming impossible to ignore. ITBench-AA puts frontier models below 50% on enterprise IT agentic tasks — the same week Cognition is valued at $26B and OpenAI is expanding agentic tooling like private MCP. Meanwhile, DeepMind's Co-Scientist is generating wet-lab-validated biology results, and specialized models like GPT-Rosalind are getting restricted-access deployment in high-stakes domains. The pattern: general agentic benchmarks look bad, but narrow domain-specific deployments are producing real results. The builders who win near-term aren't building general agents — they're going deep on a single vertical with constrained, well-defined task scopes where the model's failure modes are known and manageable.
