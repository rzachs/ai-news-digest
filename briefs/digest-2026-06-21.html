Trend to Watch

Training data provenance — meaning where the data used to build AI came from and whether it was used legally — is becoming a front-page issue, not a legal footnote. Tools that make this visible (like this database) put pressure on every AI company that built products on unlicensed content. Music is just the most emotionally resonant example; the same exposure exists for text, images, and code. This week, watch for whether any major music rights holder or artist publicly uses this database to announce a legal claim — that would signal the litigation wave is moving from threat to reality.
