Top Story

The Trump administration hit Anthropic with emergency export controls last week, forcing the company to take its two newest AI models — Claude Fable 5 and the more powerful Mythos 5 — completely offline. The order came with a 90-minute deadline and banned all foreign nationals, including Anthropic's own employees, from accessing the models. As of this briefing, both are still down. The trigger was a potential security vulnerability flagged by Amazon researchers, but the administration never fully explained the legal basis for the order. The entire AI industry is watching. Some companies are already signing backup contracts with non-US AI providers as political risk insurance.
